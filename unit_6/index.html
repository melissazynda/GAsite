Work

Platform Redesign

Company: Anaconda, Inc. (formerly Continuum Analytics)
Role: Senior UX Designer

When I joined the Anaconda team in May of 2017, they had the skeleton of an enterprise data science platform that was very much in need of design direction. In four months, I redesigned the entire product workflow and co-designed a new visual design framework that linked our pre- and post-auth experiences into one cohesive language.

Responsive E-Commerce Website

Company: Fluid
Client: Make Up For Ever
Role: UX Designer

Created a fully responsive e-commerce website for Make Up For Ever. Work included creating a site map for both the shopping and non-commercial elements; designing all pages for desktop, tablet, and mobile browsing; documenting all behaviors in a comprehensive functional specification.

Modern Licensing Experience

Company: IBM
Role: Experience Strategy

IBM SPSS Statistics is an industry leading statistical package originating in and best known for its use in the social sciences. While the product itself continues to evolve to meet changing user needs, tangential parts of the experience, such as on-boarding, have gone largely unchanged.

I worked with the SPSS Statistics team on a five month engagement to re-design, modernize, and implement a new on-boarding and license management experience, currently being piloted in the market to trial customers.
